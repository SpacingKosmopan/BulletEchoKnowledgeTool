# v1.0 Official Release!

> XX.07.2026

Hello dear [#0099ff]Bullet Echo Players[/#]!<br>
Me ([#B2FB8C]Spacing[/#]) and [#FFC106]people[/#], who helped in creating this website, are [#B2FB8C]overjoyed[/#] to announce, that completely fresh [#FF7034]Bullet Echo Knowledge Tool[/#] has been officialy released!

Our main goal was to create the ultimate resource for both [#B2FB8C]beginners[/#] and [#FFC106]hardcore players[/#]. Whether you want to check hero stats, find the best gear sets, or learn advanced strategies, we've got you covered!

### What can you find here?

- [#C4FDFF]Hero Guides[/#] – Discover tips, tricks, and the best gear setups for every hero.
- [#C4FDFF]Calculators[/#] – Calculate precise damage, armor density, and costs of upgrading heroes, drones, gears, mods or resources.
- [#C4FDFF]Mini-Games[/#] – Bored between matches? Guess maps, audio cues, crosswords, and pixelated heroes!

This is just the [#0099ff]beginning[/#] of our journey. We plan to update the tool constantly after every game patch.
